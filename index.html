Early Struggles

Steve Jobs faced many struggles in his life. He dropped out of college, was once fired from his own company Apple, and faced criticism for his ideas. Despite these setbacks, he never stopped believing in innovation.

Great Achievements

Steve Jobs co-founded Apple and revolutionized technology with products like the iPhone, iPad, and MacBook. He also transformed animation through Pixar, changing how stories are told using technology.

Learnings from Steve Jobs

  • Follow your passion, not just money
  • Failure is not the end—it’s a lesson
  • Innovation comes from thinking differently
  • Focus on quality over quantity
